For the ServiceNow Knowledge 2020 Digital Experience conference, I was the speaker for the session: `THS2122: App(s) showcase: Quality of Life items available on ServiceNow Share`<!--endexcerpt-->

Originally, I didn't think this session was approved but I was contacted late saying that they'd still like this session to go forward. I was originally approved to do a different session that then turned into a larger panel session, but after the other potential panelists backed out, the content for this session is gonna move forward in a different format (to come).

THS2122 is a quick session entirely for the benefit of the ServiceNow developer community as it highlights several items available on [share.service-now.com](https://share.servicenow.com/) that are all free to download and would make our lives a lot more easier when installed:

- [Add "Copy Field Name, Value, Display Value" to context menu for list records](https://developer.servicenow.com/connect.do#!/share/contents/1655773_add_copy_field_name_value_display_value_to_context_menu_for_list_records?t=PRODUCT_DETAILS) by me
- [Add "Copy Field Name, Value, Display Value" to context menu for form variables](https://developer.servicenow.com/connect.do#!/share/contents/1655773_add_copy_variable_name_to_context_menu_for_form_variables?t=PRODUCT_DETAILS) by me
- [Xplore: Developer Toolkit](https://developer.servicenow.com/connect.do#!/share/contents/9650888_xplore_developer_toolkit?t=PRODUCT_DETAILS) by James Neale
- [Global Code Search](https://developer.servicenow.com/connect.do#!/share/contents/8451237_global_code_search?t=PRODUCT_DETAILS) by Rick Marsha
- [Service Portal - Impersonate Dialog](https://developer.servicenow.com/connect.do#!/share/contents/6416148_service_portal_impersonate_dialog?t=PRODUCT_DETAILS) by Phyong Nguyen
- [Admin Shortcuts](https://developer.servicenow.com/connect.do#!/share/contents/6592535_admin_shortcuts1?t=PRODUCT_DETAILS) by Eric Riemer
- [Add to Update Set Utility](https://developer.servicenow.com/connect.do#!/share/contents/9824957_add_to_update_set_utility?t=PRODUCT_DETAILS) by Ben Hollifield
- [Libra Toolkit](https://developer.servicenow.com/connect.do#!/share/contents/1627055_libra_toolkit?t=PRODUCT_DETAILS) by Jarod M
- [Custom Autofill for Incidents](https://developer.servicenow.com/connect.do#!/share/contents/1655773_custom_autofill_for_incidents?t=PRODUCT_DETAILS) by me

If you are registered for the Knowledge 2020 Digital Experience, you can watch the full presentation [here](https://events.servicenow.com/widget/servicenow/knowledge2020/knowledgecatalog/session/15785038059800010GI9?#1589973335179001SIWK).
